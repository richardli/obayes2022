University of California Santa Cruz

Registration

Reduced rates are available for ISBA members and students. If you are not an ISBA member, please consider becoming a member.

To pay registration, please go to the online payment site. In order to submit payment, you need to register an IRIS account on the payment site.

The registration fee includes coffee breaks, the welcome reception, conference dinner, and lunches from Sept 7 to Sept 9.

Regular registration (until 6/30/22)
  • Students (must register as an ISBA member): $320
  • Non-student ISBA member: $370
  • Regular: $400

    • Late registration (after 6/30/22)
  • Students (must register as an ISBA member): $370
  • Non-student ISBA member: $420
  • Regular: $450


    • Travel support

    O'Bayes section and the organizing committee have secured funding from NSF and UCSC for junior researchers. Junior investigators are invited to apply for (partial) travel support. If you are a graduate student, postdoctoral trainee or young investigator (graduated with PhD no more than four years ago) and you are giving a poster you are eligible to apply. Women researchers and underrepresented groups in statistics are especially encouraged to apply. Please indicate your eligibility for the travel support in your poster submission.

    Cancellation and changes to registration

    Because of COVID-19, the registration fee paid by participants can be fully refunded (less credit card processing fee) if registration is canceled on or before July 14. 50% of registration fee can be refunded (less credit card processing fee) if canceled between July 15 and August 5. After August 5, no requests for refunds will be honored. Please note there is a 4% credit card transaction fee applied to all refunds. Transferring registration to another person is allowed at no penalty. All cancellations and substitutions must be submitted in writing. Please contact Zehang Li (lizehang@ucsc.edu).
